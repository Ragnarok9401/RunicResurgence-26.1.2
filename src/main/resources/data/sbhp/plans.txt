SBHP:
    Done:
    - Scoreboard-based health system, damage dealt to player is instead taken from this new system.
    - Natural regeneration based on a timer, heal faster based on how long ago the player was hurt.
    - Sprinting reduces healing amount by 30%
    - Nausea increases reduction to healing while sprinting (30% -> 50%)
    - Regeneration (effect) gives flat health regeneration regardless of other conditions (+3.33% of max HP healed per tick per level)
    - Strength hastens natural regeneration by 2 seconds per level after being hurt
    - Weakness delays natural regeneration by 1 second per level after being hurt
    - Some config options:
        - Natural health regeneration delay (default 5, meaning players will have to wait 5 seconds to begin regeneration after being hurt)
        - Maximum health regeneration rate time (default 20, meaning players will have to wait 20 seconds for natural regeneration to reach its peak rate)
        - Base Damage Modification (default 115, meaning all damage dealt to players will be applied with a 1.15x multiplier)
        - Respawn Health Percentage (default 80, meaning players who die will respawn with 80% of their Max Health as Current Health)
    - System Improvements:
        - A visual Damage Indicator that appears above a damaged entity
    - Survival Features:
        - Feature that allows increasing max health through consuming unique food items (bad ones included)
            > Each food item is put into its own category (Bad, Unique, Exotic, Supernatural), with each category giving specific amounts of max health (5, 10, 20, and 50 respectively)
